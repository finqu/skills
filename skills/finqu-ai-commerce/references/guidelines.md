# Guidelines

Rules and context that shape AI agent behavior in Finqu AI Commerce.

## What Are Guidelines?

Guidelines are instructions that tell the AI agent how to behave:

- What tone to use in responses
- What terms to use or avoid
- Business rules to follow
- Domain-specific knowledge

## Types of Guidelines

### Tone of Voice

```
Always respond in a friendly, professional tone.
Use "you" instead of "the customer."
Keep responses concise — max 3 sentences for simple answers.
```

### Brand Rules

```
Product names must always use title case.
Never abbreviate the brand name.
Use "collection" not "category" in customer-facing text.
```

### Business Rules

```
Never suggest discounts below 10%.
Free shipping is available for orders over €50.
Returns are accepted within 14 days.
```

### Domain Knowledge

```
Our products are handmade in Finland.
Material "birch" refers to Finnish birch wood.
Size "S" corresponds to EU 36.
```

## Best Practices

- Be specific — vague guidelines are ignored or misinterpreted
- Provide examples — show what good output looks like
- Prioritize — if guidelines conflict, the more specific one wins
- Keep it current — update guidelines when policies change
- Test — verify the agent follows guidelines in different scenarios
